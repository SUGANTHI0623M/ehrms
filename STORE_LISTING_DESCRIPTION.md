# Play Store – Full description (Store listing)

Copy the text below into **Play Console → Your app → Store presence → Main store listing → Full description**.

---

Askeva HRMS (AE HRMS) is a Human Resource Management System for modern businesses. Manage attendance, field tasks, payroll, leave, learning, and more from one app.

**KEY FEATURES**

• **Smart Attendance** – Check in and check out with GPS-verified location and selfie check-in to reduce proxy attendance.

• **Tasks & Live Tracking** – View and complete field tasks, navigate to customer locations, and share live location with your organization during task execution.

• **Payroll & Payslips** – View salary structures, monthly payslips, and deductions (PF, ESI) in one place.

• **Leave Management** – Apply for leave and track approval status in real time.

• **Onboarding** – Upload documents and complete onboarding tasks from your phone.

• **Employee Profile** – Keep your details, bank info, and documents up to date.

• **Requests** – Submit and track expenses, reimbursements, loans, and other requests.

• **Learning (LMS)** – Access courses, live sessions, and assessments on the go.

• **Performance** – View goals, self-assessments, and review feedback.

• **Announcements & Notifications** – Stay updated with company news and important alerts.

**NOTE:** This app is for employees of organizations using Askeva HRMS. Log in with the credentials provided by your HR department.

Simplify HR operations with Askeva HRMS.
